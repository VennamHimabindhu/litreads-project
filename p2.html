A Teaspoon of Earth and Sea

By Dina Nayeri

Rs.399/-

A magical novel about a young Iranian woman lifted from grief by her powerful imagination and love of Western culture. In a remote village in 1980s Iran, 11-year-old Saba Hafezi's life changes forever when her mother and twin sister, Mahtab, disappear. Saba is certain they have moved to America, where she believes everyone lives the lives of the characters in her beloved TV shows.
